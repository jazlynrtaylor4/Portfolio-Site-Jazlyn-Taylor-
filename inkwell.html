InkWell

Mission Statement

InkWell is an app that aims to make the entire process of getting a tattoo easier, from choosing a design to finding the best aftercare tips. We want to provide a one-stop shop tattoo resource app.

Problem

Users need a holistic resource that covers the tattoo process from start to finish so that they can make informed decisions about their design and artist.

Solution

InkWell is a one-stop shop that guides users through the entire tattoo process and engages them in an informative, interesting online community. It is a space for users to find answers to their questions, find inspiration, explore artist’s styles and learning more about the overall art of tattooing.

User Interviews

First Interview Findings

I conducted two sets of interviews. First I sent out a brief survey to friends on social network sites, asking general questions about their tattoo experiences. 29 people participated and the results helped me form my next set of interview questions.

Second Interview Findings

In my second round of interviews. I interviewed three individuals from different cities about their experiences with tattoos. All three have at least two tattoos. Here are my most pertinent findings: - None of the participants did any research beforehand and did not know what to expect going into their appointment - They stumbled upon their chosen artist or found them through word of mouth. - All chose designs intentionally, opting for meaningful symbols of things that they want to commemorate or celebrate forever
